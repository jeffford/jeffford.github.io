    Group Project:

  • Case Study by Lisa Miller
  • Assignment 1: (Start in class 2/10/02) Form a group of 3-4 people. You should choose a team leader, a name, and make plans for meeting outside of class.
    Read the case study. Discuss possible ways of helping Consolidated Industries and decide on a scope for the project. Produce a DataFlow Diagram of the current system that is within your chosen scope. Drill down on a few of the processes until you reach a primitive diagram. Be prepared to present your team's results during the next class time.
  • Assignment 2: As you can see, many of you chose too big a scope. Redefine your scope and redo Assignment 1 so you have a reasonable DFD! Be prepared to present your team's results during the next class time.
  • Assignment 3: Decide which parts of the system your team modeled in its current DFD should be computerized. Draw a DFD for this new system. (Big hint coming out of the discussion last week: Think small, but not so small you don't do anything!!!)Be prepared to present your team's results during the next class time.
  • Assignment 4: Choose the model you plan to computerize. Perform the 3 steps of OOA on it for the last weekend. In particular, do
    A use-case diagram
    Noun extraction and class diagram
    A state diagram for one of the classes.
    Supplying E-R diagrams and any of the other OOA documents would be very desirable.

    You will have 10 minutes to discuss your results in the last class period. There will be 5 minutes for questioning. In addition, compare and contrast structured analysis and object-oriented analysis as well as your team interactions.

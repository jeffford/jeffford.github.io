Two symbols representing 2 of the 5 groupings of the 11 cultures. Alaska is the home of 11 different Native cultures and 21 distinct indigenous languages. To tell about these, the Native Elders asked the Center to group the cultures into 5 regions based on geographic proximity and cultural similarities. The Elders chose a symbol to represent each of the 5. These are 2 of the symbols.

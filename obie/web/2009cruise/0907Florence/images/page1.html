Santa Croce Church -- A Gothic church with a 19th-century facade contains early 14th century frescoes by Giotto. The church is Franciscan, built in 1294. It wad badly damaged in 1966 when extensive flooding occurred.

It is the burial place of some of the most illustrious Italians, such as Michelangelo, Galileo, Machavelli, Foscolo, Rossini, and Marconi .

STUDIO-LAB 2

1. Look at the catalog of available photographs.

In the catalog, you can view the photographs on a contact sheet with the names under them.

You can click on the thumbnail and see an enlargement.

Do NOT download from the catalog as these are low quality files used for display purposes only. They were chosen so you could browse easily, look at them rapidly, and see several at once for comparison sake.

2. While in the catalog, note which photographs you wish to use and write their names on paper.

3. From the following menu, right click on the name and choose Save Target As to start to download a higher quality .jpg file for each one you wish to use. Save the file on your Save Drive. These files, unlike the ones in the catalog, are of varying sizes. We want you to use them as they are just .jpg versions of the original .tif files (which are huge)*.
